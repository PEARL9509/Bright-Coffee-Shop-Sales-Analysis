# Bright-Coffee-Shop-Sales-Analysis
This report examines sales trends, revenue, and customer spending behavior. It highlights top-selling products, peak periods, and spend categories using charts and pivot tables, providing actionable insights to optimize sales, inventory, and marketing strategies.

Aim of the Project

To analyze Bright Coffee Shop’s sales data to identify patterns, trends, and customer behavior, enabling the management to make informed decisions to improve revenue, optimize inventory, and enhance customer satisfaction.

Objectives

Analyze Sales Performance – Examine revenue, quantity sold, and top-selling products.
Identify Customer Spending Patterns – Categorize customers into low, medium, and high spenders.
Evaluate Peak Sales Periods – Determine the busiest days and times for better staff and inventory planning.
Assess Product Category Contribution – Identify which product categories drive the most revenue.
Provide Actionable Insights – Recommend strategies to boost sales, marketing, and inventory management.


Scope of the Project

Analysis of sales data from March 16 to present.
Focus on product categories, customer spend buckets, and store locations.
Use of charts, pivot tables, and calculated fields for visualization.
Recommendations targeted at improving sales efficiency and customer experience.
