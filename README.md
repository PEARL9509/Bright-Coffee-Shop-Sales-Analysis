Project Overview

This project analyzes sales data from Bright Coffee Shop to identify trends, insights, and patterns in customer behavior. The aim is to provide actionable recommendations to improve revenue, optimize product offerings, and understand peak sales periods.

Objectives


Clean and preprocess sales data for accurate analysis.
Identify revenue trends across different product categories and store locations.
Analyze customer spending behavior using spend bucket categories (Low, Medium, Ballers).
Highlight peak sales periods and best-selling products.
Provide insights to guide business strategy and decision-making.
Data Processing Steps
Imported raw sales dataset into Databricks.
Cleaned and transformed the dataset:
Corrected price formatting issues.
Created calculated fields, e.g., Revenue = Quantity × Unit Price.
Categorized spending into spend buckets.
Defined time intervals for transactions.
Exported the processed dataset to Google Sheets for visualization.
Built pivot tables and charts to identify trends and patterns.


Key Insights


Low spend customers make up the majority of transactions, while Ballers contribute the highest revenue per transaction.
Certain product categories consistently outperform others in revenue generation.
Peak sales occur during specific periods (days or months) that can guide marketing and inventory planning.
Revenue per store location shows variations, indicating opportunities for optimization.


Tools Used


Databricks (Data cleaning and transformation)
Google Sheets (Pivot tables and chart visualization)
Gantt charts (Project timeline tracking)
